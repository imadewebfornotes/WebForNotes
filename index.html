About Us

Hi there! I’m an independent developer, and I built this website with one simple goal—making study materials free for Bangladeshi students. When I looked around, I noticed that while there are plenty of study websites, most of them either charge money or limit what you can access. That just didn’t feel right to me. Education should be open to everyone, not something that only those who can afford it get to enjoy.

So, I decided to create a space where students can find notes, resources, and study help without any cost. Whether you’re getting ready for exams, brushing up on class topics, or just learning something new, everything here is free and easy to access.

This site is made with care and love for students like you, who work hard every day to reach their goals. My hope is that it makes your learning journey a little easier and a lot less stressful. Let’s learn, grow, and share knowledge—together, for free.
